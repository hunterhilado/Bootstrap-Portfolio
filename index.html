About me

My full name is Hunter Godfrey Hilado. I was born Januray 12th, 1997 in Dallas, TX to two wonderful parents named Michelle and Godfrey. Before I graduated High School I attended 10 different schools, spanning 5 states inclduing: Texas, West Virginia, Tennessee, Alabama and Florida. I have 1 sibling named Heath born in 2003. Moving around a lot was tough because no one likes being the new kid, but i look at it with a more optimistic view. I played many sports growing up but Lacrosse and MMA were my two favorites. I am a brown belt in Taekwondo, winning 5 gold medals in varying international tournaments I attended. I am also a blue belt in jiu-jitsu.
